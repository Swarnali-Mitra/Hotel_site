Explore Nearby Attractions

Step outside The Bengal Haven and discover the cultural and scenic gems of South Kolkata.

Kalighat Temple

One of the most revered temples in India, dedicated to Goddess Kali. A must-visit spiritual destination just 5 minutes from the hotel.

Rabindra Sarobar Lake

A serene lake surrounded by lush greenery — ideal for morning walks, jogs, or peaceful boat rides. Just 10 minutes away.

Gariahat Market

Experience the local buzz at this busy marketplace — from street food to sarees, electronics to jewelry. A shopper’s paradise close by.

Victoria Memorial

A stunning white-marble monument from the British era, surrounded by gardens. Great for photos and learning about Kolkata’s past. 20–25 minutes from hotel.
