Dining at The Bengal Haven

From authentic Bengali delicacies to rooftop continental delights, experience culinary bliss at our hotel.

The Calcutta Café

Savor classic Bengali dishes in a warm, heritage-style dining space.

  • Macher Jhol – Traditional Bengali fish curry
  • Shorshe Ilish – Hilsa fish in mustard gravy
  • Chingri Malai Curry – Creamy coconut prawn curry
  • Mishti Doi – Sweetened yogurt dessert

The Rooftop Terrace

Dine under the stars with stunning views of the Kolkata skyline.

  • Grilled Chicken with Herb Butter
  • Thai Green Curry with Jasmine Rice
  • Veg Sizzler Platter
  • Tiramisu – House-special dessert
